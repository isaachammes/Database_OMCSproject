Online Master's in Computer Science Programs

Data Info:

I collected the data on 3/17/2020 by Google searching until I could not find any new programs.
For-profit colleges were intentionally left out. Here are notes about what each column shows:

  • In State/Out of State Tuition: Tuition information is the most up to date that I could find and
    does not necessarily include fees.
  • Total Cost: Calculated by simply multiplying the Out of State Tuition by the Required Credits.
  • Essay: Whether a statement of purpose/other essay is required or not.
  • GRE: Whether the GRE is required or not. This is based on a student with a bachelor's in a
    related degree, no work experience, and above a 3.0 gpa.
  • Recommendations: Number of letters of recommendation that are required for admission.
  • Concentrations: Concentrations listed on school website. General means none were clearly
    stated.
