Online Master's in Computer Science Programs

Instructions:

To search for a program, simply enter the maximum tuition cost per credit hour (this is based
on out-of-state tuition rates), select if GRE is required for admissions, and enter the maximum
number of recommendation letters required for admissions(integer from 0-3).
Note: All fields are required to search. To view all programs, leave "Maximum Tuition" blank.
To view the concentrations available for their program, simply enter the name of the university
as it appears in the "Programs" table and click "Show Concentrations". If you want to see the
full list of concentrations, leave the field blank.

Data Info:

I collected the data on 3/17/2020 by Google searching until I could not find any new programs.
For-profit colleges were intentionally left out. I did my best to collect accurate data, but
it may not be perfect. The "Essay" column could be an essay or statement of purpose. The GRE
Yes/No is based on an individual who has over a 3.0 gpa, a relevant bachelor's, and no work
experience. The tuition information is based on 9 credit per semester pricing if the college
charged different prices per credit based on the number of credits taken. Information on
concentrations was hard to find for some colleges, so I only listed specific concentrations
if the college website had it clearly stated. Only fully online master's in computer science
degrees were included (no software engineering, data science, IT, etc.). I hope this information
can be helpful!
